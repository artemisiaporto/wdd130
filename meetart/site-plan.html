Home page

We are encouraged to have different social media to share about different subjects. Saving photos and texts in so many places could take much work because we need to choose the correct writing model for such a social network and make the necessary changes to each one of the texts we will share. On this site, I can share different topics starting from the same point. You are now invited to know about my work and education, and my personal time. And the best part is: you choose from which you want to start!

Work and Education

I am enrolled in the bachelor's degree in Geophysics at the University of São Paulo, where I develop a Scientific Initiation project about nanomagnetic particles. I am being trained at a company called EEG (Engineering and Environment, Geophysics). There I develop new skills and reinforce the knowledge I learned in college. I worked as an intern at Planetarium do Carmo, from September 2021 to June 2022.
