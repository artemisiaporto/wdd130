Other Marine Animals

In addition to fish, there are many super interesting Pokémon that were inspired by marine animals. Here we present three of them.

Mantine

A Pokémon based on manta ray. Their sizes vary from 5.5 meters to 7.5 meters, being very large. In the anime, there as scenes where the Remoraid, another Pokémon fish, swims attached to the Mantine undersides.

Lapras

This Pokémon resembles the Plesiosauria, an extinct marine reptile. According to science, this animal lived on Earth, between the Triassic and Cretaceous periods.

Horsea

Based on the seahorse, it also looks like a dragon. This similarity is derived from the Japanese legend which tells that when a seahorse lives for a century it becomes a dragon.
