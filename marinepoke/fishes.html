Fishes

Stunfisk

An electric and ground Pokémon type has as its origin the Stargazer, a fish that buries itself in the sea bottom and can make some electric discharges when disturbed.

Lanturn

An electric and water Pokémon type. Its origin came from the Footballfish and the dolphins. The fish has a light ball up on its head and the dolphin is related to Apollo, the Greek sun god who has the dolphin as his signature animal.

Sharpedo

It is based on shark and ocean sunfish. Both have the skin like sandpaper. Its body also remembers a torpedo, giving him a “license” to be a very fast Pokémon.

Now you know more about Pokémon related to fish and you can talk about it with someone! If you would like to know more about marine Pokémon, go to the page “Other Marine Animals.
